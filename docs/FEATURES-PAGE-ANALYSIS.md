## 🔍 COMPETITIVE RESEARCH

### Competitors Analyzed:

**1. Typeform**
- **Pricing:** $25-74/month
- **Key Features:** Beautiful UI, one question per page, logic jumps, payment collection
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, limited free plan (10 responses/month), no WhatsApp
- **Target:** Global, design-focused businesses

**2. Google Forms**
- **Pricing:** FREE
- **Key Features:** Simple, unlimited responses, Google Workspace integration
- **Weaknesses:** Basic design, limited customization, no webhooks, no WhatsApp
- **Target:** Basic use cases, education

**3. JotForm**
- **Pricing:** $34-99/month
- **Key Features:** 10,000+ templates, payment integration, HIPAA compliance
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, complex interface, limited free plan (100 responses/month)
- **Target:** Enterprises, healthcare

**4. Other Players:**
- **Formstack:** $50+/month, enterprise-focused
- **SurveyMonkey:** $25+/month, survey-focused
- **Wufoo:** $14+/month (Discontinued by SurveyMonkey)
